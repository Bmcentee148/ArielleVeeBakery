The baker behind it

Hi, I'm Arielle.

Arielle Vee Bakery is a small, home-based bakery on Long Island built on one simple idea — that the sweetest part of any celebration should be made just for you.

Every cookie, cake pop, cupcake and cake is baked from scratch and hand-decorated to match your colors, your theme, and your occasion. No two orders are ever quite the same, and that's exactly the point.

Whether it's a bridal shower, a first communion, a birthday, or a "we survived the week" treat, I'd love to make something sweet for your table.

— Arielle
